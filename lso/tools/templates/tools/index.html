Tools

Transliteration

Sanscript transliterates text from one script to another. Sanscript supports all Indian scripts and the most popular romanizations. It focuses on Sanskrit, but it has strong support for other languages.

Dictionaries

The Monier-Williams Sanskrit-English Dictionary is the largest and most commonly used Sanskrit-English dictionary. Dictionary data comes from the Cologne Digital Sanskrit Lexicon project.
