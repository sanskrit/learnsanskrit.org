Sanscript transliterates text from one script to another. Sanscript supports all Indian scripts and the most popular romanizations. It focuses on Sanskrit, but it has partial support for other languages.

It's easy to use Sanscript:

  1. Choose your options.
  2. Put text in the left box.
  3. Click "Run".
