Common letters

Usually, students try to memorize the consonants in order, starting with {{ 'ka'|d }} and ending with {{ 'ha'|d }}. This is slow and unrewarding. Some consonants are so rare that they appear in Sanskrit only rarely. Others are so common that they appear in almost every other word. How to memorize a letter

If you are having trouble memorizing a letter, consider the students of Japanese and Chinese, who have to memorize thousands of characters. To help them remember particular characters, these students create vivid stories about the different letters that relate to their shape. These stories serve as reminders of how different letters sound.
