And that is all! Starting from ordinary Sanskrit sounds, we have built up our knowledge of how Sanskrit works. We learned that verbs describe some action and that nouns describe the different parts of that action. We also learned that adjectives describe nouns and that uninflected words have various functions.

Most importantly, we have built a strong foundation for exploring the rest of Sanskrit. Now the lessons will become much more interesting. Soon we will start reading lines from the Bhagavad Gita.
