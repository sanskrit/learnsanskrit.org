{% from 'guide/macros-devanagari.html' import rows %} The famous `om` symbol is a combination of three separate letters. It has the first part of {{ 'a'|d }}, the last part of {{ 'U'|d }}, and the `candrabindu` on top: {{ rows('OM') }} This symbol is most frequently used to mark the beginning of a text or recitation. It is especially common in the Upanishads, some of which explore the sounds in {{ 'OM'|d }} quite thoroughly.
