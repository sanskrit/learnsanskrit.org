Review ------ We can use *pronouns* to refer to nouns in general: !!! examples - {{ m.x('naro gacchati -> sa gacchati', 'The man goes. -> He goes.') }} Asking questions ---------------- We can ask questions by using `kim`, a pronoun that mean something like "who?" or "which?". It is used almost exactly like `tad`: !!! examples - {{ m.x('[kaM] pazyasi', '[What] do you see?') }} - {{ m.x('[kena] carati', '[With whom] do you walk?') }} `kim` differs from `tad` in two small ways. First, `kaH` blends normally, unlike `saH`: !!! examples - {{ m.x('[ko] gacchati') }} Second, `kim` is used instead of `tat`: !!! examples - {{ m.x('[kiM] khAdAmi', 'What am I eating?') }}
