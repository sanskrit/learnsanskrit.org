Other stems ----------- All of the nouns we've studied have ended in `-a`: !!! examples - {{ m.x('narAn pazyasi', 'You see the men.') }} But stems can end in many other ways. And each stem ending has its own set of endings. Here are just a few examples: !!! examples - {{ m.x('agni -> agniH', 'fire') }} - {{ m.x('vAyu -> vAyuH', 'wind') }} - {{ m.x('bhagavat -> bhagavAn', 'The blessed one') }} - {{ m.x('dhanus -> dhanuH', 'Bow') }} The natural reaction here is despair. But there's no need for it. Noun endings follow a few basic patterns. And by finding these patterns, we can quickly learn how to use a new stem. The basic patterns ------------------ In theory, we could look at thousands of nouns and try to figure out the basic patterns. But this would take too long.
