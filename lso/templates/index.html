learnsanskrit.org was created to help people learn Sanskrit as quickly and easily as possible. The heart of the site is a comprehensive grammar guide that covers nearly every part of the language:

Go to the grammar guide

The site also contains some useful tools for working with Sanskrit:

  • Sanscript lets you transliterate Sanskrit text. Sanscript supports nearly every Indian script and a variety of romanizations.

  • The Monier-Williams dictionary is the largest Sanskrit-English dictionary in the world. The version hosted on the site is (in my opinion) especially pleasant to use.
